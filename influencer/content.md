# **FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

*The questions every creator asks — answered.*

**Q: Do I need a minimum follower count to apply?**

A: No minimum follower count is required — especially for UGC creators. For influencer campaigns, we work with creators at every tier: nano (1K+), micro (10K–100K), macro (100K–1M), and mega (1M+). Engagement quality and content authenticity matter more than follower numbers.

**Q: How long does the approval process take?**

A: Our team reviews every application within 48 business hours. You'll receive an email \+ WhatsApp message with your approval status and next steps. Once approved, your creator dashboard goes live immediately.

**Q: How and when do I get paid?**

A: Payment is processed within 15 working days after brand sign-off on delivered content. We pay via direct bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS) or UPI. All payment terms are outlined clearly in each campaign brief before you accept it.

**Q: What is a barter collaboration?**

A: Barter collabs mean you receive free products from the brand in exchange for content — no cash involved. It's ideal for newer creators, lifestyle unboxing niches, or D2C product testing. Some barter deals convert to paid partnerships over time.

**Q: Can I opt into both Paid and Barter campaigns?**

A: Yes — and we recommend it\! Selecting 'Both' in your preferences doubles your campaign matches and keeps your content calendar full. You're never obligated to accept any campaign — every brief is a choice.

**Q: Can I work with multiple brands at once?**

A: Absolutely. You can accept multiple non-competing campaigns simultaneously. Your manager will ensure there are no conflicts before confirming any deal. Most active creators run 4–8 campaigns per month.

**Q: What platforms does the network support?**

A: Instagram (Reels, Stories, Posts), YouTube (Long-form & Shorts), LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Moj, ShareChat, and Podcasts. You can list all active platforms in your profile and receive relevant campaigns for each.

**Q: What is the NDA and why do I need to sign it?**

A: The NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) protects both you and the brands you'll work with. It covers campaign confidentiality, IP rights, content standards, and payment terms. It's a standard industry practice that ensures professional relationships and legal clarity for everyone.

**Q: Is this only for Indian creators?**

A: Our primary network focuses on Indian and South Asian markets. However, creators making content for Indian audiences globally — including diaspora communities in UAE, USA, UK, and Singapore — are welcome to apply.

**Q: What happens after I submit the application?**

A: Our team reviews your profile within 48 hours. If approved, you'll receive a welcome email with your dashboard login, your creator tier confirmation, and a set of matched campaign briefs ready to accept.

# **TONE OF VOICE & COPY GUIDELINES**

**Brand Voice Pillars:**

* Confident, not arrogant  *— We know we're the best — we don't need to shout it*

* Warm, not corporate  *— Talk like a creator who made it, not a boardroom memo*

* Direct, not aggressive  *— State the value clearly. Let the numbers do the heavy lifting*

* Premium, not exclusive  *— Anyone can join — but not everyone gets matched*

**Copy Rules:**

* Always lead with the creator's benefit — not the brand's need

* Use real numbers wherever possible (7,800+, 8X, 15 days, 450+)

* Keep sentences short. One idea per sentence.

* Use em-dashes and fragments for rhythm — like this

* Never use passive voice in headlines

* CTAs should feel like an invitation, not a command

* Use 'You' and 'We' — never 'Creators' or 'The Agency'
