_Gap addressed:_ PID lists no contact details. Competitors (Plixxo, Grynow, Chtrbox) all show a support email in the footer. ### Trust & credentials

**Q3:** Can you share any quantitative statistics about your creator network — such as: number of creators currently in your network, total number of brand campaigns executed, total payout made to creators to date, or approximate number of brands you have worked with?

_Gap addressed:_ All 5 competitors lead with network statistics (creator count, brand count, campaign count) as the primary trust signal. Kofluence leads with "750K+ creators, 3,500+ campaigns"; Grynow with "30,000+ campaigns, 1,500+ brands". Brilliant Brains' page cannot compete without its own credibility numbers. Even approximate figures ("50+ brand campaigns", "200+ creators onboarded") are usable._

---

**Q4:** What is your typical creator payout timeline — how many days after a campaign goes live (or content is approved) does a creator receive payment?

_Gap addressed:_ This is the single biggest weakness across ALL 5 competitors — none state a clear payout timeline except Plixxo ("within a month"). A specific, prominently displayed payout commitment ("Creators paid within 30 days of content approval") is the clearest differentiator available. Without confirmation from Brilliant Brains, we cannot include this critical trust signal._

**Q5:** Which brands has Brilliant Brains run influencer campaigns for — can you share a list of 8–15 brand logos we can use on the page? Please confirm we have permission to display these logos.

_Gap addressed:_ All 5 competitors feature a brand logo wall as their central credibility element. Domain_skill.md ethical note states logos may only be used for brands the agency has genuinely worked with (ASCI compliance). We need the confirmed client brand list before the Designer Agent can build the logo wall._

**Q7:** What is the minimum follower or subscriber count required to join Brilliant Brains' creator network — and does this threshold differ by platform (Instagram vs YouTube vs LinkedIn)?

Without a confirmed threshold, the FAQ and form cannot be completed accurately._
