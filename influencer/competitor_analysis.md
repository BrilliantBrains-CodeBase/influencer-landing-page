## Cross-competitor patterns

### Sections that appear in 3+ competitors
- Header with CTA (all 5): "Sign Up" / "Join" / "Apply Now" button in sticky header — mandatory
- Hero with influencer-aspiration headline (all 5): "Monetise your content", "Build your brand", "Turn influence into income"
- Benefits / Why Join section (all 5): 3–6 icon cards covering brand access, payout, support
- Brand Logo Wall (all 5): Greyscale logos of recognisable brand clients — mandatory trust section
- Creator Testimonials (4/5): Quotes with creator name + niche + follower count
- Platform Statistics / Credibility numbers (all 5): Creator count, brand count, campaign count
- How It Works / 3-step process (4/5): Registration → Matching → Earn
- Registration Form or App Sign-Up (all 5): Primary conversion element
- Niche / Category Coverage (4/5): Tags or cards showing content verticals covered

### Trust signals that appear in 3+ competitors
- Creator network size (number of creators): All 5 competitors — most prominent trust signal
- Brand client logos / Fortune 500 names: All 5 competitors
- Years in operation / campaign volume: 4/5 competitors (Chtrbox, Kofluence, Grynow, Pulpkey)
- Minimum follower threshold stated: 3/5 (Plixxo: 1K IG; Pulpkey: 500 YT/2K IG; Kofluence: any social presence)
- Platform coverage (Instagram, YouTube, etc.): All 5 competitors

### CTA patterns that appear in 3+ competitors
- "Sign Up" / "Join Now" in header + hero: All 5 competitors
- Free / no-cost messaging: 2/5 (Plixxo "Sign Up Free", Kofluence "Download Free")
- Download App CTA: 1/5 (Kofluence only — app-centric model)
- Dual CTA (header + hero): All 5 competitors

### Common weaknesses across all competitors (opportunity for our client)
1. **No payout timeline**: NOT ONE competitor clearly states "you get paid within X days". Plixxo comes closest ("within a month") but buries it. A prominent payout guarantee/timeline is a major differentiation opportunity.
2. **No FAQ section on most**: Only Kofluence and Plixxo have visible FAQ sections. Most competitors leave common questions unanswered — influencers drop off.
3. **No earnings showcase**: No competitor shows a creator's actual earnings, campaign rate card, or earning potential examples. "What can I actually earn?" is the top question every influencer has.
4. **Generic testimonials**: Testimonials exist but rarely show earnings or campaign names. No competitor shows a creator saying "I earned ₹50,000 from my first campaign with them."
5. **WhatsApp absent**: None of the five competitors prominently feature a WhatsApp contact or floating WhatsApp button — yet WhatsApp is the primary B2C trust channel for Indian creators.
6. **No ASCI / compliance mention**: None address influencer disclosure compliance — a regulatory gap that an agency could turn into a trust signal by proactively addressing it.
7. **Niche exclusivity not addressed**: No competitor addresses "what if I'm already with another agency?" — creators with existing agency relationships are left without an answer.
