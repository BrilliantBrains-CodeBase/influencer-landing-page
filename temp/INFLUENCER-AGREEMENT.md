### **2\. Definition of Confidential Information**

“Confidential Information” includes, but is not limited to:

* Campaign briefs, concepts, strategies, and creative direction  
* Unreleased product details, pricing, offers, or launch timelines  
* Internal business data including marketing strategies, operations, and financial insights  
* Any communication (written, verbal, or digital) exchanged between the Agency, Brand, and Influencer  
* Any non-public information shared as part of the collaboration

All such information shall be treated as strictly confidential.

---

### **3\. Obligations of the Influencer**

The Influencer agrees to:

* Not disclose, publish, or discuss any Confidential Information prior to the approved go-live date  
* Not share campaign-related information with any third party including brands, agencies, media, or individuals  
* Maintain complete confidentiality of all materials, briefs, and communications shared by the Agency  
* Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of fulfilling the agreed collaboration

---

### **4\. Communication Protocol (Strict & Binding)**

* The Influencer **shall not, under any circumstances, directly contact or communicate with the Brand**  
* All communication including approvals, queries, negotiations, clarifications, or feedback must be conducted **only through the assigned Point of Contact (POC) from the Agency**  
* The Agency shall remain the **sole and exclusive communication channel** between the Influencer and the Brand

Any violation of this clause will be considered a **serious breach of this Agreement**, and the Agency reserves the right to take **strict action**, including termination and legal recourse.

---

### **6\. Permitted Disclosures**

The Influencer may disclose Confidential Information only to:

* Their manager, legal advisor, or authorized representative

Provided that such individuals are informed of the confidential nature of the information and agree to be bound by similar confidentiality obligations.

---

### **7\. Duration of the Agreement**

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **1 Year** from the date of signing, or until such Confidential Information becomes publicly available through no fault of the Influencer, whichever is earlier.

---

### **9\. Intellectual Property Rights**

* All content created as part of this collaboration may be used, reproduced, edited, and promoted by the Brand and/or Agency across platforms including advertisements, digital media, and marketing campaigns  
* The Influencer grants the Agency and Brand a non-exclusive, royalty-free right to use such content

---

### **10\. Consequences of Breach**

In the event of a breach of this Agreement by the Influencer:

* The Agency reserves the right to immediately terminate the collaboration  
* Any pending payments or benefits may be withheld  
* The Influencer may be held liable for damages, losses, or legal consequences arising from the breach  
* The Agency may initiate legal action in case of serious violations

---
